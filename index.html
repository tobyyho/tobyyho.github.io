With a voice as haunting as it is powerful, 21-year-old Angel Luvos is carving her own path in the dark pop scene. Inspired by the synthpop sounds of Depeche Mode, alternative and R&B elements of Shaker and Dutch Melrose, and mainstream pop flavours of Ness Barret, Tate McRae, and Billie Eilish, Angel Luvos blends cinematic production with deeply immersive, evocative lyrics.
Her sound is a reflection of her unique ability to merge shadowy, atmospheric soundscapes with pop melodies, creating music that's both introspective and expansive.
A natural-born storyteller, Angel Luvos' songwriting takes center stage in her work, with lyrics that explore themes of love, loss, and self-discovery. She brings a rare depth to her artistry, channeling her experiences and emotions into songs that resonate with listeners on an intimate level.
Though Angel Luvos is known for her dark pop persona, her songwriting goes far beyond the music she releases under her own name, exploring multiple genres and topics in her broader catalog.
